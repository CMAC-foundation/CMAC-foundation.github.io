About Us

Built on Equality,
Sustainability & Purpose

CMAC Community Development Foundation is a women-led NGO and social enterprise on a mission to solve three interconnected crises — menstrual poverty, gender inequality, and environmental damage from plastic waste.

We operate a dual-arm model: our enterprise arm sells biodegradable banana pads commercially, while profits fund our NGO arm to supply schools and underserved communities at subsidized and flexible payment rates. Self-sustaining, community-rooted, and built to last.

Women Employment
All production staff are women from Kisii and surrounding communities. CMAC creates formal employment, fair wages, and economic independence for women who previously had no formal sector access.
📚
Girls' Education
By supplying affordable and free pads to schools, we directly tackle the leading cause of school absenteeism among girls — ensuring every girl can attend class every day of the month.
🌱
Environmental Protection
Replacing plastic pads with fully biodegradable alternatives removes millions of pads from Kenya's waste stream annually. Our circular production model uses banana waste — creating value from what is discarded.
